About

Based in Seattle, Washington, I’m a software developer with a focus in data science and a strong foundation in public health. I’m a proud UW Husky and graduate with a degree in Public Health, where I was first introduced to informatics and data-driven research. That experience sparked my passion for technology and inspired me to further develop my skills through The Tech Academy's Data Science Boot Camp.


I am now fully trained in full-stack development and experienced in programming languages and data visualization tools. I’m excited to bring this unique blend of public health insight and technical skill to mission-driven work and creative tech solutions.

I’ve worked on a range of projects that showcase both my software development and data science skills.
